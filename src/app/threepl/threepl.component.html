Our 3PL Services

As a Third-party (3PL), ZHE provides flexible, optimal and efficient support solutions to meet the need of customers who do not have the capacity to respond to dynamic customer demands. This solution is aimed at reducing and managing their core operating costs and lead times so that our customers can outsource their non-core competences activities.

Our Third-Party Logistics services model include the activities of providing solutions for our customers in relation to Logistics and Distribution. The packages of services as provided under this solution: include (but can also be customized depending on the kind of service required by the customer):

  • TRANSPORTATION

  • REVERSE LOGISTICS
WAREHOUSING
ZHE intends to understand the value flow of materials for its customers, that way we provide a perfect warehousing solution that is aligned and rationalized with the process of our customers.Our operational capacity provides a one-stop-service
point that covers an end-to-end life cycle of warehousing which includes high quality warehousing operations (order receiving,order management, stock keeping and management, inventory management, returns management).

To provide solution to our customers, ZHE provide the following warehousing solutions:

Dedicated Contract Warehousing

ZHE provides a dedicated warehousing solution to customers who seek independent and dynamic solutions. This includes recommending, stipulating, obtaining dedicated facilities, solutions development and implementation, project management, process improvement, recruitment and training.

Shared Warehousing

ZHE provides a solution to customers whom their needs do not need large capacities. This solution provides the flexibility and cost savings by allotment of resources (space, labor, equipment and transportation) while meeting customersâ€™ requirements at the same time.

Temperature Controlled Solutions

ZHE provides solutions to cater for temperature regulation requirements for any kind of consignments (Life sciences and General Goods) on storage and distribution solutions, which provide for all temperature specific needs.

Community Dock Warehousing

Synchronized activities intended to avoid storage by instantly receiving processing and dispatching consignments immediately to receivers.

Pick & Pack Warehousing

warehouse environment designed for effeicient processing of smaller and fast moving good orders.

Value Added Warehousing
Postponement

ZHE provides a solution to accomodate delaying final product configuration until the very last moment. This gives provision to manage and reduce inventory and provide a more reponse to dynamic product demand.

Kittling Services

ZHE also provides kittling solutions to accomodate communication of more than one consignments in a given amount of stock keeping unit (SKU).

Hazardous Material Storage

ZHE provides a solution for Warehousing chemical (including flammables, corrosives, oxidizers, gases, poisons,etc) consignments that meet the standards as required by the regulations.

INDUSTRIAL PROJECTS TRANSPORTATION

ZHE treats Out-of-gauge transportation as projects, regardless of the size. We provide logistical support to transport these kinds of irregular sized pieces from any place to any place. This solution forms part of our Freight Extra services with cater for irregular handling.

Our support also specialises in managing sourcing and procurement processes for our customers, helping them to acquire or outsource products and services.

ZHE also recognises the logistical support of returning goods and products. This include management returns from customers and returns to suppliers.
